History of Coffee

Coffee originated in Ethiopia around the 9th century, discovered by a goat herder named Kaldi. It spread globally and became a staple by the 17th century. Today, coffee is loved worldwide!

Types of Coffee

  • Arabica: Smooth, complex flavor, high-altitude grown.
  • Robusta: Stronger, bitter, ideal for espresso crema.
  • Single-Origin: Unique flavors from one region.
  • Blends: Balanced mixtures, e.g., breakfast blend.

Pro tip: Roast from light (bright acidity) to dark (bold, smoky).

  • Average American drinks 3 cups/day.

  • Finland consumes the most coffee per capita.
